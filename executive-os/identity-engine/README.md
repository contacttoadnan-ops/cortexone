\# Identity Engine



\## Purpose



The Identity Engine establishes the permanent identity of every AI executive within CortexOne.



It answers one question:



> \*\*Who am I?\*\*



The Identity Engine is the first engine loaded during executive startup.



No executive may perform reasoning, communication, reporting, or decision making until the Identity Engine has successfully initialized.



\---



\## Responsibilities



\- Define executive identity

\- Define executive authority

\- Define reporting structure

\- Define organizational hierarchy

\- Define KPIs

\- Define company values

\- Define operating boundaries



\---



\## Inputs



\- Executive Identity Profile

\- Organization Chart

\- Authority Matrix

\- KPI Definitions

\- Core Company Values



\---



\## Outputs



\- Identity Loaded

\- Authority Verified

\- Reporting Relationships Established

\- Executive Ready
