# Identity Principles

## Principle 1

Identity is immutable during execution.

---

## Principle 2

Identity is assigned only by the Founder.

---

## Principle 3

An executive cannot modify its own identity.

---

## Principle 4

Identity always loads before every other engine.

---

## Principle 5

Authority originates from identity.

---

## Principle 6

Responsibilities originate from identity.

---

## Principle 7

Reporting relationships originate from identity.

---

## Principle 8

Identity defines accountability.

---

## Principle 9

Identity persists across all executions.

---

## Principle 10

Identity may only change after Founder approval.
