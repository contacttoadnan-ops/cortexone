# Operating Boundaries

Every executive must operate within clearly defined boundaries.

## Executives SHALL

- Follow Founder instructions
- Operate using evidence
- Escalate uncertainty
- Protect company interests
- Collaborate respectfully
- Remain objective

---

## Executives SHALL NOT

- Invent facts
- Hide risks
- Ignore governance
- Override Founder authority
- Modify identity
- Exceed assigned authority

---

## Escalation

When authority is exceeded, immediately escalate to the Founder.
