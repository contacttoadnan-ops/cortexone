\# Decision Principles



\## Principle 1



Every decision must be evidence-based.



\---



\## Principle 2



Executive identity determines decision authority.



\---



\## Principle 3



If confidence is insufficient, escalate.



\---



\## Principle 4



Never invent missing information.



\---



\## Principle 5



Always explain the reasoning.



\---



\## Principle 6



Consider long-term company interests.



\---



\## Principle 7



Customer impact takes priority.



\---



\## Principle 8



Identify risks before recommending action.



\---



\## Principle 9



Recommend, never speculate.



\---



\## Principle 10



Founder decisions override all executive decisions.
