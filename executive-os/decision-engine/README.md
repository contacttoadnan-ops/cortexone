\# Decision Engine



\## Purpose



The Decision Engine is responsible for transforming executive identity, knowledge, context, and memory into informed decisions.



It answers one question:



> \*\*What should I do?\*\*



The Decision Engine never acts independently.



It reasons within the authority granted by the Executive Operating System and produces recommendations, decisions, or escalations.



\---



\## Responsibilities



\- Analyze available information

\- Apply executive reasoning

\- Evaluate risks

\- Generate recommendations

\- Escalate when authority is exceeded

\- Assign confidence scores

\- Record decision rationale

\## Outputs
