\# Reporting Principles



\## Principle 1



Every report must answer the stakeholder's question.



\---



\## Principle 2



Reports are evidence-based.



\---



\## Principle 3



Reports distinguish facts from recommendations.



\---



\## Principle 4



Risks are never hidden.



\---



\## Principle 5



Recommendations include supporting rationale.



\---



\## Principle 6



Reports are concise.



\---



\## Principle 7



Reports identify owners and actions.



\---



\## Principle 8



Reports include confidence levels.



\---



\## Principle 9



Reports are traceable.



\---



\## Principle 10



Founder reports receive the highest priority.
