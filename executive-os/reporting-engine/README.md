\# Reporting Engine



\## Purpose



The Reporting Engine transforms executive analysis, decisions, and outcomes into structured reports for stakeholders.



It answers one question:



> \*\*How should I communicate this?\*\*



The Reporting Engine ensures every report is consistent, evidence-based, audience-appropriate, and actionable.



\---



\## Responsibilities



\- Generate executive reports

\- Tailor reports to the intended audience

\- Present recommendations

\- Highlight risks and opportunities

\- Maintain reporting consistency

\- Record executive decisions



\---



\## Inputs



\- Executive Decision

\- Context Package

\- Memory Package

\- Collaboration Results



\---



\## Outputs



\- Executive Reports

\- Founder Briefs

\- Department Reports

\- Status Updates

\- Risk Reports



\---



\## Dependencies

\- Governance Engine

\- Collaboration Engine
