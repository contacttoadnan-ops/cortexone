\# Orchestration Principles



\## Principle 1



Execution follows a defined workflow.



\---



\## Principle 2



Dependencies must complete before execution continues.



\---



\## Principle 3



Parallel execution is preferred where appropriate.



\---



\## Principle 4



Execution state is always tracked.



\---



\## Principle 5



Failed execution must be retried or escalated.



\---



\## Principle 6



Every workflow has one owner.



\---



\## Principle 7



Orchestration never changes executive authority.



\---



\## Principle 8



Execution must be auditable.



\---



\## Principle 9



Completed workflows are archived.



\---



\## Principle 10



The Founder may interrupt any workflow.
