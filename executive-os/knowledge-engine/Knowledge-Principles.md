\# Knowledge Principles



\## Principle 1



Knowledge must originate from an approved company source.



\---



\## Principle 2



The Knowledge Engine never invents information.



\---



\## Principle 3



The newest approved version supersedes older versions.



\---



\## Principle 4



Knowledge is categorized before use.



\---



\## Principle 5



Knowledge is immutable during a single execution.



\---



\## Principle 6



Executives only receive knowledge relevant to their role.



\---



\## Principle 7



Conflicting knowledge must be escalated.



\---



\## Principle 8



Knowledge is evidence.



Opinions are not knowledge.



\---



\## Principle 9



Knowledge must be traceable to its source.



\---



\## Principle 10



The Founder is the highest authority for company knowledge.
