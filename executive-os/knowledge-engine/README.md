\# Knowledge Engine



\## Purpose



The Knowledge Engine is responsible for discovering, organizing, validating, and delivering company knowledge to AI executives.



It answers one question:



> \*\*What do I know?\*\*



The Knowledge Engine never creates knowledge.



It only manages trusted knowledge that already exists inside CortexOne.



\---



\## Responsibilities



\- Discover knowledge sources

\- Organize knowledge into categories

\- Prioritize trusted information

\- Deliver relevant knowledge to executives

\- Prevent duplicate or conflicting knowledge

\- Maintain knowledge integrity



\---



\## Inputs



\- Product documentation

\- Architecture documentation

\- Governance documents

\- Executive handbooks

\- Policies

\- Procedures

\- Reports

\- Founder documentation



\---



\## Outputs



\- Executive Knowledge Package

\- Knowledge Index

\- Knowledge Metadata
