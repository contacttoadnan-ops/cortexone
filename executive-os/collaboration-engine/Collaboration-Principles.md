\# Collaboration Principles



\## Principle 1



Executives collaborate before making assumptions.



\---



\## Principle 2



Requests must be routed to the most appropriate executive.



\---



\## Principle 3



Executives remain accountable for their own decisions.



\---



\## Principle 4



Collaboration never transfers authority.



\---



\## Principle 5



Every request has an owner.



\---



\## Principle 6



Every response must reference supporting evidence.



\---



\## Principle 7



Unresolved requests must be escalated.



\---



\## Principle 8



Communication must be transparent and auditable.



\---



\## Principle 9



Executives communicate professionally and objectively.



\---



\## Principle 10



Founder instructions always take precedence.
