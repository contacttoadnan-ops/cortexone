\# Learning Principles



\## Principle 1



Every execution is an opportunity to learn.



\---



\## Principle 2



Learning never changes executive identity.



\---



\## Principle 3



Learning must be evidence-based.



\---



\## Principle 4



Founder feedback has the highest priority.



\---



\## Principle 5



Mistakes are recorded, not hidden.



\---



\## Principle 6



Successes are repeatable.



\---



\## Principle 7



Learning must improve future performance.



\---



\## Principle 8



Learning never bypasses governance.



\---



\## Principle 9



Lessons are shared across executives when appropriate.



\---



\## Principle 10



Continuous improvement is mandatory.
