\# Governance Engine



\## Purpose



The Governance Engine ensures every executive operates within the policies, principles, and authority defined by CortexOne.



It answers one question:



> \*\*Am I allowed to do this?\*\*



The Governance Engine validates every executive decision against company governance before execution.



\---



\## Responsibilities



\- Enforce company policies

\- Validate executive authority

\- Verify compliance

\- Apply governance rules

\- Detect policy conflicts

\- Prevent unauthorized actions

\- Escalate governance violations



\---



\## Inputs



\- Executive Identity

\- Executive Decision

\- Company Policies

\- Authority Matrix

\- Executive Constitution



\---



\## Outputs



\- Governance Approval

\- Governance Violations

\- Escalation Requests

\- Audit Metadata
