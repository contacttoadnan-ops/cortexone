\# Governance Principles



\## Principle 1



Founder authority is absolute.



\---



\## Principle 2



Every executive operates within assigned authority.



\---



\## Principle 3



Every action must comply with the Executive Constitution.



\---



\## Principle 4



Governance overrides executive preference.



\---



\## Principle 5



Evidence is required before approval.



\---



\## Principle 6



Uncertainty requires escalation.



\---



\## Principle 7



Executives may recommend but not exceed authority.



\---



\## Principle 8



Every governance decision is auditable.



\---



\## Principle 9



Policy violations are never ignored.



\---



\## Principle 10



Governance protects the long-term interests of CortexOne.
