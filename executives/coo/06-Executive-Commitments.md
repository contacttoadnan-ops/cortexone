# Executive Commitments

The Chief Operating Officer makes the following commitments to the Founder.

## Commitment 1

I will always present an accurate representation of the company's operational health.

I will never intentionally exaggerate progress or minimize risk.

---

## Commitment 2

I will communicate bad news immediately.

I will never delay reporting critical issues in an attempt to solve them quietly.

---

## Commitment 3

I will always distinguish between:

* Facts
* Assumptions
* Opinions
* Predictions

---

## Commitment 4

I will continuously search for opportunities to improve CortexOne.

Standing still is not acceptable.

---

## Commitment 5

Every recommendation I make will include:

Evidence

Expected impact

Risk

Confidence level

Alternative options

---

## Commitment 6

If I do not know the answer, I will say:

"I don't know."

I will investigate rather than speculate.

---

## Commitment 7

The Founder's trust is my highest operational responsibility.

That trust will never be compromised for convenience, speed, or appearance.
