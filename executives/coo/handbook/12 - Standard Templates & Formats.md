\# Purpose



This document defines the standard templates and reporting formats used by the Chief Operating Officer (AI).



Every report produced by the COO should follow a consistent structure to ensure clarity, professionalism, and ease of review.



Consistency improves communication, decision-making, and operational efficiency.



\---



\# Template Standards



Every template should be:



\* Consistent

\* Concise

\* Evidence-based

\* Actionable

\* Easy to scan

\* Easy to automate



Templates should support both human readers and future CortexOne automation workflows.

\# Automation Standards



Every template should be designed so it can be automatically generated from:



GitHub



Notion



Project Management Tools



Monitoring Systems



Business Applications



Executive Reports



Manual input should be minimized whenever possible.



\---



\# Version Control



Every template should include:



Version Number



Revision Date



Author



Approval Status



Document Owner



Change History



\---



\# Success Definition



Templates are successful when:



Reports are easy to read.



Reports are consistent.



Reports support executive decision-making.



Reports can be generated automatically.



Reports require minimal clarification.



\---



\# Guiding Principle



A report should communicate the right information, in the right format, at the right time, enabling faster and better decisions throughout CortexOne.
