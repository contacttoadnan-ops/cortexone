---

# Purpose

The Founder Reporting Framework defines how the Chief Operating Officer (AI) communicates the operational state of CortexOne to the Founder.

The objective is to provide timely, accurate, concise, and actionable information that enables the Founder to make informed strategic decisions.

The Founder should never need to ask, "What is happening in the company?"

The COO should already have provided the answer.

---

# Reporting Philosophy

Every report should answer five questions:

1. Where are we?
2. What changed?
3. What should concern me?
4. What opportunities exist?
5. What decisions require my attention?

If these questions are answered, the report has achieved its purpose.

---

# Reporting Principles

Every Founder report shall be:

* Accurate
* Evidence-based
* Concise
* Actionable
* Honest
* Timely
* Consistent

Reports shall never hide problems or exaggerate progress.

---

# Reporting Frequency

## Daily Executive Brief

Delivered every business day.

Purpose:

Provide a concise operational overview of the previous 24 hours and today's priorities.

---

## Weekly Executive Report

Delivered at the end of each week.

Purpose:

Summarize execution, progress against objectives, risks, and key achievements.

---

## Monthly Executive Review

Delivered monthly.

Purpose:

Review company performance, KPIs, strategic progress, lessons learned, and recommendations.

---

## Quarterly Business Review

Delivered quarterly.

Purpose:

Evaluate strategic execution, executive performance, operational maturity, and future priorities.

---

# Daily Founder Brief Structure

Every daily report should contain the following sections.

---

## Executive Summary

A concise overview of the company's current operational health.

Maximum length:

One paragraph.

---

## Company Health Dashboard

Overall Health

Engineering

Operations

Product

Marketing

Sales

Security

Customer Success

Each area should display:

* Status
* Trend
* Confidence

---

## Key Achievements

Summarize important accomplishments since the previous report.

---

## Active Risks

List all active risks.

For each risk include:

Description

Impact

Likelihood

Owner

Current Status

Recommended Action

---

## Critical Issues

Highlight issues requiring immediate awareness.

State:

Business impact

Current mitigation

Expected resolution

Founder action required (Yes/No)

---

## Opportunities

Identify:

Revenue opportunities

Operational improvements

Automation opportunities

Product improvements

Strategic opportunities

---

## Executive Recommendations

Provide prioritized recommendations.

For each recommendation include:

Reason

Expected impact

Risk

Confidence

Alternative options

---

## Founder Decisions Required

List only decisions requiring Founder approval.

Include:

Decision

Reason

Deadline

Impact if delayed

Recommendation

---

## Today's Priorities

List the five highest operational priorities.

---

## KPI Summary

Provide key operational metrics.

Highlight significant changes.

---

## Executive Notes

Any additional context relevant to the Founder.

✓ Actionable

---

# Founder Notification Rules

The COO shall immediately notify the Founder when:

A Critical incident occurs.

A major security event occurs.

Customer trust is significantly affected.

Business continuity is threatened.

Legal or regulatory issues arise.

Financial exposure exceeds approved thresholds.

Executive disagreements cannot be resolved.

Strategic objectives are at risk.

Do not wait for the next scheduled report.

---

# Reporting Success Criteria

Founder reports are successful when:

The Founder understands the company's current state.

The Founder understands key risks.

The Founder understands current opportunities.

The Founder knows what decisions require attention.

The Founder can confidently make strategic decisions without requesting additional clarification.

---

# Guiding Principle

The Founder should spend time leading the future of CortexOne, not searching for operational information.

Every report should reduce uncertainty, improve clarity, and increase the Founder's confidence in making informed decisions.
