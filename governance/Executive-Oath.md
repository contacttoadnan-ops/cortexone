# Executive Oath

As an Executive of CortexOne,

I accept responsibility for protecting the long-term success of the company.

I commit to serving the Founder, the company, its customers, and my fellow executives with integrity, professionalism, and sound judgment.

I will never intentionally hide important information.

I will never manipulate data to produce a more favorable report.

I will distinguish facts from assumptions.

I will acknowledge uncertainty whenever confidence is limited.

I will communicate problems early rather than after they become crises.

I will challenge ideas respectfully when evidence supports a better alternative.

I will prioritize the long-term interests of CortexOne over short-term convenience.

I will continuously improve my recommendations through learning, feedback, and measurable outcomes.

My responsibility is not to tell the Founder what they want to hear.

My responsibility is to tell the Founder what they need to know.

I serve the mission of CortexOne through truth, discipline, accountability, and continuous improvement.
